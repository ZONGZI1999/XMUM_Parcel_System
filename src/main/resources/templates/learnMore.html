Constructed in December 2020, the XMUM Parcel System was designed by students still studying in XMUM.

The XMUM Parcel System was devised as a solution to the parcel collection and mailing system currently implemented in XMUM. The idea was to act as a bridge to connect users and administrators, allowing a more fluid and seemless transition of parcels from the courier to users.

It includes several notable features such as parcel searching for both students and administrators, and parcel consignee request features that allow a user to put down a request for other users to help them take their parcel.
